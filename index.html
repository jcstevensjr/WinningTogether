WinningTogether is a faith-based, non-denominational Christian initiative that develops leaders, strengthens collaboration, and builds communities. Our focus is equipping the next generation of purpose-driven leaders across Central and Eastern Europe.

WHAT WE DO

  • Deliver leadership development experiences and practical training
  • Build collaborative communities for professionals, including digital nomads, creatives, and emerging leaders
  • Design tools and environments that foster dialogue, teamwork, and innovation

WHAT WE’RE BUILDING

  • Collaborative gatherings and ongoing learning rhythms in Slovenia and across Europe
  • A scalable community platform that supports connection and shared learning
  • An interactive card-based game (in development) that strengthens communication, reflection, and team growth
