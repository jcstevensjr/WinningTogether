Collaboration. Leadership. Shared impact.

WinningTogether is a faith-based, non-denominational Christian initiative focused on leadership development, collaboration, and community building to equip the next generation of leaders across Central and Eastern Europe.

What we do

  • Leadership development experiences and practical training
  • Community-building for professionals (digital nomads, creatives, emerging leaders)
  • Tools that spark meaningful dialogue and teamwork

What we’re building

  • Collaborative gatherings and learning rhythms in Celje and beyond
  • A scalable community platform for connection and shared learning
  • An interactive card-based game (in development) to support dialogue and team growth
